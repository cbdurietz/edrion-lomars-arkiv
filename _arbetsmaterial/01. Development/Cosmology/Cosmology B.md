## General System Notes

- All planets orbit prograde (same direction)
- All planetary orbital planes are within 2° of each other
- Aeteras B's orbital plane is tilted 6.2° relative to the planetary plane
- The barycenter of the binary system lies approximately 0.2 AU from the center of Aeteras A
- Aeteras III and IV maintain their 60° separation through a 1:1 orbital resonance
